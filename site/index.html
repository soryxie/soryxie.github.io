Bio

I am a final-year undergraduate student in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), also currently a research intern at Horizon Robotics. I was advised by Prof. Zhouhan Lin at SJTU and Yichen Gong at Horizon Robotics. My research interest covers multimodal deep learning, speech technology and self-supervised learning. I will join Cheriton R. Computer Science School at University of Waterloo and Vector Institute in Fall 2022, advised by Prof. Wenhu Chen.
